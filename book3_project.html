Overviews
  • "The Forty Rules of Love" is a captivating novel that explores the themes of love, spirituality, and self-discovery. The story follows Ella, a middle-aged woman, who embarks on a journey of enlightenment as she reads a manuscript about the legendary poet Rumi and his spiritual guide, Shams of Tabriz. Through the novel, the forty rules of love unfold, offering profound wisdom on human relationships and divine love.
